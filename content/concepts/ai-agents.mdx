## What are AI Agents?

AI agents are autonomous systems that use LLMs to plan, execute, and learn from actions to achieve goals.

## Why It Matters

AI agents automate complex multi-step workflows, reducing manual effort by 60% and enabling 24/7 operations.

## How to Implement

1. Define clear objectives and success criteria
2. Choose agent framework (LangChain, AutoGPT)
3. Implement tool access (search, code, APIs)
4. Add human oversight checkpoints
5. Monitor and log all actions

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What can AI agents automate?**
Research, coding, data analysis, customer service, and administrative tasks.

**Are AI agents safe?**
Safety depends on human oversight and tool access controls.
