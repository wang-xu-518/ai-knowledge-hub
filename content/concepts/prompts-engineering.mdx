## What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt engineering crafts effective inputs to guide AI models toward desired outputs.

## Why It Matters

Effective prompts improve AI output quality by 50%, reduce token usage, and enable consistent results.

## How to Implement

1. Define clear objectives for AI output
2. Use structured formats (XML, JSON)
3. Provide context and examples
4. Iterate based on outputs
5. Document successful prompts

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is few-shot prompting?**
Providing examples in the prompt to guide AI behavior.

**How do I reduce hallucinations?**
Use specific constraints, request citations, and implement verification steps.
