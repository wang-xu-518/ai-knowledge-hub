## What is Fine-tuning?

Fine-tuning adapts pre-trained AI models to specific tasks or domains by training on curated datasets.

## Why It Matters

Fine-tuning improves task accuracy by 15-30%, enables brand voice consistency, and reduces API costs.

## How to Implement

1. Collect and prepare training data
2. Select base model (GPT-4, Claude, Llama)
3. Configure fine-tuning parameters
4. Train and evaluate performance
5. Deploy fine-tuned model

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much data is needed for fine-tuning?**
Minimum 100-500 examples; optimal is 1,000-10,000 examples.

**How long does fine-tuning take?**
Training typically takes 1-24 hours depending on dataset size.
