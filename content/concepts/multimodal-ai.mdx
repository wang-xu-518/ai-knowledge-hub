## What is Multimodal AI?

Multimodal AI processes and generates multiple data types including text, images, audio, and video.

## Why It Matters

Multimodal AI enables more natural human-AI interaction, powers vision-based automation, and supports accessibility tools.

## How to Implement

1. Identify multimodal use cases
2. Select appropriate model (GPT-4V, Claude, Gemini)
3. Design user interface for multiple inputs
4. Handle cross-modal consistency
5. Test with diverse data types

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What are examples of multimodal AI?**
GPT-4V for image analysis, DALL-E for image generation, Whisper for speech-to-text.

**Is multimodal AI available via API?**
Yes, OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google provide multimodal APIs.
