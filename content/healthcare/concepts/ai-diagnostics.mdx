## What is AI Diagnostics?

AI Diagnostics refers to the use of artificial intelligence algorithms to assist medical professionals in identifying diseases and conditions from medical images, lab results, and patient symptoms.

## Why It Matters

AI diagnostics can analyze medical images with accuracy comparable to or exceeding human radiologists, reduce diagnostic errors, and accelerate detection of life-threatening conditions.

## How to Implement

1. Assess current diagnostic workflows
2. Select FDA-cleared AI diagnostic tools
3. Integrate with existing PACS/RIS systems
4. Train staff on AI-assisted workflows
5. Monitor performance metrics

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is AI Diagnostics FDA cleared?**
Yes, several AI diagnostic tools have received FDA clearance including IDx-DR for diabetic retinopathy and Viz.ai for stroke detection.

**How accurate is AI diagnostics?**
Modern AI diagnostic systems achieve 90-95% sensitivity and specificity in controlled studies.
