## What is Medical RAG?

Medical RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) combines large language models with medical knowledge bases to provide evidence-based clinical answers.

## Why It Matters

Medical RAG helps clinicians access the latest research, reduces time spent on literature review, and supports evidence-based decision making.

## How to Implement

1. Curate medical literature databases
2. Deploy RAG infrastructure
3. Implement citation tracking
4. Validate responses against clinical guidelines
5. Monitor for hallucinations and errors

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the accuracy of Medical RAG?**
Accuracy depends on knowledge base quality; typically 85-95% for well-curated sources.

**How does Medical RAG handle citations?**
Medical RAG systems provide source citations allowing clinicians to verify information.
