## What are Electronic Health Records (EHR)?

EHR systems are digital versions of patient paper charts that contain medical history, diagnoses, medications, and immunizations.

## Why It Matters

EHR systems improve care coordination, reduce medical errors, and provide real-time access to patient data across healthcare providers.

## How to Implement

1. Evaluate EHR vendors and their HIPAA compliance
2. Plan data migration from paper records
3. Train staff on new digital workflows
4. Establish interoperability standards
5. Implement regular backups and security

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does EHR implementation cost?**
Costs range from $500/month for small practices to $50,000+ for enterprise hospital systems.

**Is cloud-based EHR HIPAA compliant?**
Yes, when using BAA-covered cloud providers with proper encryption and access controls.
