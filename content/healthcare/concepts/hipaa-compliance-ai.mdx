## What is HIPAA Compliance for AI?

HIPAA compliance for AI ensures that artificial intelligence systems handling protected health information (PHI) meet regulatory requirements.

## Why It Matters

Non-compliance can result in penalties up to $1.5 million per violation and loss of patient trust.

## How to Implement

1. Conduct HIPAA risk assessment
2. Implement encryption for data at rest and in transit
3. Deploy access controls and audit logs
4. Establish BAA agreements with vendors
5. Train staff on PHI handling

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What AI tools are HIPAA compliant?**
Only AI vendors with signed BAAs and proper security certifications.

**What happens if AI exposes PHI?**
Organizations must report breaches within 60 days and may face OCR investigations.
