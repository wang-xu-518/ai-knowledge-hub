## What is Telehealth AI?

Telehealth AI combines remote healthcare delivery with artificial intelligence to provide automated triage, monitoring, and clinical decision support.

## Why It Matters

Telehealth AI extends healthcare access to rural areas, reduces ER visits, and enables 24/7 health monitoring for chronic conditions.

## How to Implement

1. Select HIPAA-compliant telehealth platform
2. Integrate AI triage and symptom checker
3. Deploy remote monitoring devices
4. Train patients on self-service tools
5. Establish escalation protocols

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Telehealth AI covered by insurance?**
Coverage varies; Medicare and many private insurers now cover telehealth services.

**What conditions can be managed via Telehealth AI?**
Chronic disease management, mental health, dermatology, and urgent care for non-emergency conditions.
