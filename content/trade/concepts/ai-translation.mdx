## What is AI Translation?

AI translation uses neural machine translation to convert text between languages with high accuracy for business communication.

## Why It Matters

AI translation enables cross-border communication, reduces localization costs by 80%, and accelerates market entry.

## How to Implement

1. Assess translation volume requirements
2. Select AI translation provider (DeepL, Google Translate)
3. Fine-tune for industry-specific terminology
4. Implement human review workflows
5. Measure accuracy and iterate

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is AI translation accurate enough for legal documents?**
AI translation requires human review for legal, medical, and financial documents.

**Which languages does AI translation support?**
Modern NMT supports 100+ languages with varying accuracy levels.
