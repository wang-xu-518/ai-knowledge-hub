## What is AI Supply Chain?

AI supply chain optimization uses machine learning to forecast demand, optimize inventory, and streamline logistics.

## Why It Matters

AI supply chain reduces inventory costs by 20%, minimizes stockouts, and improves delivery times.

## How to Implement

1. Integrate data sources (ERP, WMS, supplier APIs)
2. Deploy demand forecasting AI
3. Implement inventory optimization
4. Automate supplier communication
5. Monitor key logistics metrics
