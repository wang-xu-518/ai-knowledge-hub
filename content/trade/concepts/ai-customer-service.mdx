## What is AI Customer Service?

AI customer service uses chatbots and virtual assistants to handle customer inquiries, order tracking, and support tickets.

## Why It Matters

AI customer service reduces response times from hours to seconds, handles 70% of routine inquiries, and operates 24/7.

## How to Implement

1. Map common customer journeys
2. Deploy AI chatbot on website/messaging platforms
3. Integrate with CRM and order management systems
4. Train on company knowledge base
5. Establish human handoff protocols

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What languages does AI customer service support?**
Most platforms support 40+ languages with real-time translation.

**Can AI handle complex customer issues?**
AI handles routine issues; complex cases are escalated to human agents.
