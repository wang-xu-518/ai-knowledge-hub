## What is AI Product Sourcing?

AI product sourcing uses machine learning to discover suppliers, compare pricing, and validate product quality.

## Why It Matters

AI sourcing reduces research time by 70%, identifies verified suppliers faster, and enables data-driven procurement decisions.

## How to Implement

1. Define product requirements and specifications
2. Deploy AI sourcing platform (Alibaba, Jungle Scout)
3. Filter suppliers by ratings, certifications
4. Request quotes and compare offers
5. Validate samples and references

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Which AI sourcing platforms are most popular?**
Alibaba.com, Jungle Scout, and Keepa for Amazon sellers.

**How does AI verify supplier legitimacy?**
AI analyzes verification badges, response rates, transaction history, and customer reviews.
